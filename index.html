Our Purpose

Village Rising was founded on a simple, powerful idea: that a connected community is a thriving community. We bridge the gap for families who need support but may not qualify for traditional aid, creating a network of neighbors helping neighbors.

Our work is rooted in integrity, service, and the belief that everyone deserves the opportunity to flourish. We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to making a tangible impact.

How We Serve

We offer a range of programs designed to meet families where they are and provide the tools for sustainable growth.

Community Hubs

Safe, welcoming spaces for families to connect, share resources, and attend workshops on financial literacy, parenting, and career development.

Resource Navigation

One-on-one assistance to help families access essential services like childcare, housing support, and healthcare, simplifying complex systems.

Youth Mentorship

Connecting young people with positive role models in the community to foster skills, confidence, and a vision for their future.
