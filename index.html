Our Mission is Connecting Families. Building Futures.

Village Rising's mission is to close the gap between crisis and stability through integrated childcare, navigation, financial literacy, and housing support by rebuilding the modern day village and creating pathways to family stability, economic mobility, and community leadership, one family at a time.

Where connection becomes collective growth.

Join Our Mission

Our Purpose

Village Rising was founded on a simple, powerful idea: that a connected community is a thriving community. We bridge the gap for families who need support but may not qualify for traditional aid, because of income requirements.

Our work is rooted in integrity, service, and the belief that everyone deserves the opportunity to flourish. We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to making a tangible impact.

How We Serve

We offer a range of life advancing programs designed to meet low-to-moderate income families. Our offer the following programs:
1. Overnight and Drop-In Childcare
2. Essential Family Resource Center
3. Housing Stability Program
4. Workforce Development & Internship Program


For inquires before applying please email info@ourvillagerising.org. Please allow up to 7 business days for a response.

VR Childcare and Resource Center

Our program provides a nurturing, educational environment for children while offering parents a unique fill in the gap program for essential family support and community resources. We combine high quality childcare with access to professional workshops and advocacy services that aims to empower the entire family to grow and succeed together.


To apply please email info@ourvillagerising.org

Housing Stability

We facilitate temporary and emergency housing solutions by offering families immediate housing support. This includes a comprehensive suite of resources ranging from legal mediation to personalized budgeting workshops designed to prevent eviction. This intervention-based approach that addresses the systemic causes of housing insecurity, ensuring that vulnerable households have a stable foundation for professional and personal growth.


To Qualify for our housing stability program, the following is required: 1. Proof of income 2. Proof of Housing Emergency 3. Background Screening. Persons with sexual, drug and domestic violence related charges are not eligible for this program


Please submit the required documentation to info@ourvillagerising.org This is a waitlisted program. Services are not Guaranteed.

Live Scan & Access to Employment Services

Village Rising's Live Scan program supports low-income individuals and families by removing common administrative barriers to employment and professional advancement. We provide access to required background checks and related document services, such as mobile notary support, that are often prerequisites for jobs, licensing, housing, and voluneteer opportunities. Our goal is to ensure that cost, process complexity, or lack of access does not prevent individuals from moving forward with employment or stability-building opportunities.


To apply please email info@ourvillagerising.org

Workforce Development & Internship Program

Village Rising’s Workforce Development Program offers structured internship and skill-building opportunities for college students, emerging professionals, and community members seeking meaningful pathways to growth. Through hands-on experience, mentorship, and real-world exposure, participants develop practical skills, professional confidence, and a clearer understanding of their career direction. The program is designed to strengthen workforce readiness while creating equitable opportunities for individuals and families to build sustainable futures.


For internship or workforce opportunities submit your resume to info@ourvillagerising.org
